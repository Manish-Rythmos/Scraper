Symptoms

  • I am interested in using Unity Collaborate and would to partake in the Open Beta.

Cause

You would like to join the Unity Collaborate Open Beta.

Resolution

To join the Beta, go to the Collaborate home page and click 'Join now' to download the latest Unity build with Collaborate.

More Information

We recommend using the most recent version, but if you need access to Unity Collaborate in 5.4, please contact us.
