Symptoms

  • I am unable to activate the "In App Purchasing" service.

Cause

You may not be an Owner or Manager of the Organization your project is managed by.

Resolution

IAP and other Unity Services can be enabled by an Organization's project Managers and Owners. Members with the "User" role do not have this permission. Please contact support for additional help enabling IAP.
