Symptoms

  • I am trying to generate stats reports for my game and need to find my API key .

Cause

 You are looking to acquire your API key in order to generate Monetization Stats .

Resolution

1. Go to and log in to your Ads Dashboard.

2. Click "API keys".

3. Copy the API key to your clipboard or click "Generate new key".
