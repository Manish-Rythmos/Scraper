Symptoms

  • I have submitted my invoice to accountspayable-fi@unity3d.com and have not yet received my wire transfer.

Cause

It will take 30 days for the funds to be released.

Resolution

Once you have invoiced us, it will take 30 days from receipt of the invoice for us to release the funds. Allow a few business days for the wire transfer to come through.

Send invoices to the email address specified in this document (accountspayable-fi@unity3d.com), not to any personal email addresses. The ensures invoices are recorded and processed properly.
